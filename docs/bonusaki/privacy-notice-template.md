# Bonusaki Privacy Notice Template

Use this on the QR landing page, poster, or cafe counter card. Adapt it to the
merchant and local legal requirements before launch.

## Short customer notice

By scanning this Bonusaki QR code, you can play a scratch-and-win reward
campaign for [Cafe Name]. We process the information needed to run the campaign,
validate rewards, prevent duplicate redemption, and measure campaign results.

If you enter your email, it may be used for reward delivery and, only where you
consent, cafe marketing. Rewards are single-use and may be checked by cafe staff
before fulfillment.

For privacy questions, contact [Cafe privacy email] or BI Solutions Group at
[support email].

## Data processed

- reward code and redemption status
- campaign, merchant, and QR batch identifiers
- technical event data such as scan, play, reward view, and redemption events
- email address if the customer submits it
- hashed or minimized identifiers where possible

## Purposes

- run the scratch-and-win campaign
- issue and validate single-use rewards
- prevent fraud and duplicate redemptions
- produce campaign reports for the merchant
- send marketing only when the customer has consented

## Retention

Suggested pilot retention:

- event logs: 90 days
- reward/redemption records: 12 months
- customer email marketing list: merchant policy, consent-based

## QR poster microcopy

Scan to play Bonusaki for [Cafe Name]. Rewards are single-use and subject to
cashier validation. If you enter your email, it is used for this campaign and
marketing only if you consent.
