This template is a commercial starting point, not legal advice. Have a local
lawyer review it before repeated use.

## Pilot package

Provider will configure and operate a Bonusaki QR scratch-and-win campaign for
the merchant.

Included:

- one merchant campaign
- QR batch for the agreed number of placements
- reward rules and probability setup
- cashier validation instructions
- basic launch support
- campaign summary report

Not included unless written in the order form:

- custom POS integration
- payment processing
- SMS sending costs
- physical printing costs
- Apple Wallet or Google Wallet production issuer setup
- automated self-service merchant dashboard

## Pricing

Recommended starter terms:

- setup fee: EUR 490, due before setup starts
- monthly pilot fee: EUR 149 per month
- minimum term: 3 months
- extra campaign: EUR 150 each
- extra location: EUR 99 per month
- QR print design: EUR 120 one-time
- physical printing: cost plus 25% handling margin

Payment terms:

- invoices payable within 7 days
- campaign launch can be paused for overdue invoices
- rewards are paid or fulfilled by the merchant, not by Provider

## Merchant responsibilities

Merchant must provide:

- approved reward list and prize inventory
- daily reward limits
- reward expiration policy
- cashier instructions and cashier contact
- privacy notice placement approval
- staff training time before launch
- prompt notification of abuse, fraud, or customer complaints

Merchant is responsible for honoring valid rewards and for complying with local
consumer, advertising, tax, and food-service rules that apply to the rewards.

## Provider responsibilities

Provider will:

- configure the campaign using the agreed rules
- generate QR codes and campaign assets
- provide cashier validation instructions
- monitor pilot issues during business hours
- provide a campaign report at the agreed interval
- keep reward tokens and admin access private

## Customer data

The pilot may collect customer email addresses only when the campaign rules and
privacy notice say so. Customer emails should be used only for the agreed
campaign and follow-up marketing consent.

Provider stores reward tokens, redemptions, and event logs needed to run the
pilot. Customer email values should be hashed or otherwise minimized where
possible.

## Fraud and abuse

Merchant agrees that:

- each reward is single-use
- cashier must validate reward status before fulfillment
- screenshots should not be accepted as final proof without validation
- suspicious redemptions can be refused pending review
- Provider may pause a QR batch or campaign if abuse is detected

## Cancellation

Either party may cancel after the minimum term with 14 days written notice.
Provider may suspend the campaign immediately for non-payment, abuse, illegal
activity, or material security risk.
