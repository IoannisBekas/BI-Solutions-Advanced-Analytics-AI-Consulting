# Terms And Privacy

## Public Terms

The Open Risk & Resilience Atlas is an independent research and briefing tool. It is provided for country screening, source discovery, donor mapping, and finance-context analysis. Source links are included for due diligence and follow-up analysis.

## Data Sources

The public atlas uses open public datasets and links back to source publishers where possible. Source publishers retain their own licenses, terms, and update schedules. Users should consult the original source before republication or operational use.

## No Official Endorsement

References to public institutions, UN agencies, development banks, donors, or data publishers do not imply endorsement of this atlas.

## Privacy

The static public GitHub Pages atlas does not require user accounts and does not intentionally collect personal data.

## Data Scope

The public static site uses the public datasets listed in the source scope and links back to source publishers where possible.
